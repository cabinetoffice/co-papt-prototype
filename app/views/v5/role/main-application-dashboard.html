Why we collect diversity data

We are committed to recruiting people that reflect the diverse communities we serve. To do this we need your help in filling out these monitoring questions. You can select ‘prefer not to say’ for any questions you do not want to answer.

The information you provide in the diversity monitoring and more about you sections will:

  • not be used as part of the selection process
  • not be seen by the interview panel
  • only be used by the department, the Commissioner for Public Appointments and the Cabinet Office for statistical purposes

No information will be published which allows any individual to be identified.

Your responses are collected by the Cabinet Office and the government departments managing applications. The data is used to produce management information about the diversity of applicants. Your information will also help ensure that our recruitment processes are fair to all. Read more about your data and diversity data.
